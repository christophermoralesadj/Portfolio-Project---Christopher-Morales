About Me

What brought me to this point?

My name is Christopher Morales. I've been a teacher for the past 13 years. This October I decided to resign from my position as a teacher. Though I enjoyed what I was doing I felt like it was time for a change. After much research I decided to enroll in Nucamp's front-end bootcamp and to my surprise, I really enjoyed what I was doing.

What's my educational background?

I currently hold a Bachelor's degree in Finance. As mentioned above, I'm currenly enrolled in Nucamp's Coding Bootcamp. Since starting, I've decide to enroll in the Full-stack Bootcamp instead of just the font end.the great thing about being a former teacher is that you learn how to find answers. In my short exposure to web developement, I've learned that one of the most important skills you can have is not knowing everything but being able to find answers when you don't know something.

What makes me a great candidate?

I have demonstrated skills and solid fundamentals and I can add value for your company. I still have a lot to learn but I'm a quick learner, ready to roll up my sleeves and not afraid of challenges. My goal is to grow my Web Developer skills and learn from the senior developers so that I can provide even more value to your business.

What's my motivation?

Simply put, my family. I enjoy spending time with my family and would like to provide them with a good life.
